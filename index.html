Double Plus Room

here’s a reason why our Double Plus room is so popular – it’s big and open enough for a two-person slumber fest, and it’s got a private bathroom, complete with a powerful shower, fresh towels, hairdryer and complimentary toiletries. Still not enough? Well, we’ve also thrown in a 42” large-screen TV for all your channel-surfing needs, as well as free coffee and tea, and soft and hard pillow options for the best sleep ever.

Our Double Plus room is so comfortable, you might not want to leave it. But if you do step out of your room, as our guest, you have full access to our open-air pool, fitness corner and the Library.

Solo Room

What’s in a name? Well, in this case, everything. Our Solo rooms are perfect for on-to-go jet-setters who are travelling by themselves. Every room comes with a cushy and comfy single bed (perfect for those all-important power naps in between flights), as well as a desk and 32” TV so you can catch up on all your favourite reality shows. You’ll also find fresh towels and toiletries in your room, as well as a powerful shower in the shared bathroom, which is just a few steps from your door. This shared bathroom becomes your own private space once you’ve stepped in, so do try to keep those shower-time karaoke sessions short!

Feel free to explore our premises once you’ve settled into your Solo room. As our guest, you have full access to our open-air pool, fitness corner and the Library.
